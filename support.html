FAQ

  1. How do i register for the poster making competetion?
    -> For the registration, first you have to login. if you don't have an account, just signup! After you are logged in, you will find the register option.
  2. How do i get information about the judges ?
    -> Just go to the "about" section in our "Home" page. All the information about us including the judges

  3. When will the results get declared? -> After the registrations are over , all the participants shall recieve the date of declaration of results through the e-mail id that they have provided.
  4. How do i change the poster that i have already submitted? -> You can change the poster through the "edit" option that is provided after you are logged in. Note that you change your poster only if the registrations are still going on. i.e., once the registrations are closed, you can't edit the poster.
  5. How do i contact you virtually? -> Just go to the "Home" page , then the "Contact us" page. You'll find the necessary .
  6. Where can i see the previous year's exhibits? -> Go to the "Home" page. Then to the "posters " page. you'll find the necessary.
  7. How many members should be there in a team for registering for the competetion? -> A team can consist of 3 members max and 1 member least ( 1 main designer and 2 codesigners if 3 ).
  8. Is affiliation necessary for registration for the competetion ? -> Yes.
  9. Which is the last date to submit the poster? -> After you register for a particular competetion , you'll be notified the last date for submission of the poster according to the topic you choose.
  10. If you have any question other than the ones mentioned above, do mail us your question. We'll try to reply as soon as we can
